I am a data scientist with a deep passion for research in computer vision and solving complex social problems using algorithms and machine learning techniques. I am currently a Project Assistant at [Jio Institute](https://www.jioinstitute.edu.in/), where I focus on developing AI-based solutions for biomedical imaging.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from Vivekanand Education Society's Institute of Technology, Mumbai. My research interests include the application of computer vision in medical imaging, natural language processing, and the development of algorithms to address social and environmental challenges.

Most recently, I was a research intern at [K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering, Mumbai](https://www.somaiya.edu/en/view-member/160111/) under Dr. Jagannath Nirmal, where I developed a multi-resolution analysis framework using computer vision techniques to detect Crown-of-Thorns Starfish in coral reef environments, resulting in significant improvements in environmental monitoring and a publication in the *Journal of Intelligent Marine Technology and Systems (IMTS)*. Prior to that, I served as a Software Development Intern at [Ernst & Young (EY)](https://www.ey.com/en_in/services/risk), where I integrated machine learning models into risk management systems and developed AI-driven solutions for natural language processing tasks, enhancing data analytics for banking compliance. My journey into research began at the [Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata](https://www.isical.ac.in/~bibl/), working under Dr. Pradipta Maji, where I focused on applying computer vision techniques in biomedical imaging to improve cancer detection accuracy through advanced color normalization methods. These experiences have deepened my passion for using algorithms to address real-world challenges in both environmental and healthcare contexts.

When I am not in front of a computer screen, I have a keen interest in playing professional soccer and enjoy planting new plants in my surroundings every weekend, nurturing nature while contributing to a greener environment.
